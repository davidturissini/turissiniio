Meet David

Welcome to turissini.io. My name is David Turissini, and this is where I post my travels and adventures. I love travelling, hiking and backpacking. I recently got my PADI open water certification and I also occassionally dabble in mountaineering. I take any chance to experience the outdoors and live life to the fullest.

Meet Melissa

Since Melissa and I met in 2011, we have been flying around the country together experiencing a lot of what this diverse nation has to offer. Melissa is currently a graduate student in Seattle but will be moving down to California in the next few months.

Why a blog?

I love sharing my adventures with other people, and hopefully my stories and experiences can be a resource for other people who share my interests.
